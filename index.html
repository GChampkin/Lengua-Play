¡Bienvenidos!

Founded in 2024, Lengua Play is an interactive site designed to provide learners of Spanish with a fun environment in which to learn and play with those hard-to-master topics you just can't get your head around.

As you browse through our games you will find that they provide you with some tips and tricks and helpful reminders of key things to consider in relation to each topic. Your knowledge will then be put to the test in an interactive game where you can practice your skills - the fun way!

Following the "Play" link will take you to our playground where you will find what you're looking for. So click, learn and play! ¡Disfruta!

A note from the author

Hi! My name's Gregory Champkin and I am a secondary languages teacher specialising in Spanish and French education in London, United Kingdom. I have been a teach for 4 years and have extensive experience in helping the younger generations to "get" languages. I know first-hand how bumpy the journey can be to understanding a new language and so, as a lover of fun games, I created this website to provide a more enjoyable way for learners to wrap their heads around some of the trickier concepts in the Spanish language.

Learning languages wasn't easy for me either and I did not learn them the conventional way. As an ex-performer, I moved to Paris, France in 2016 on a performance job. There I realised how necessary it was for me to communicate in other languages. I got a private coach in both French and Spanish and started my journey from there.

It wasn't easy but I can tell you one thing for free, it's your commitment and "can-do" attitude that will make all the difference! It took me 2 years from A to B to become fluent. By 2019, thanks to nothing other than my determination I managed to get on a university course to achieve a PGCE in Secondary Languages education.

I made languages a part of my life, and so can you! So keep learning, be inquistive, and never give up. Oh, and always remember to have fun with it!
